MAJOR HIGHLIGHTS

Strategic Portfolios.

Jockey Distribution

Leo Group (headquartered in Kuttanellur, Thrissur, with facilities in Ollur) is the official Super Distributor for Jockey (Page Industries) across all of Kerala.

This prestigious portfolio is managed directly under the Leo Group Team by specific managers dedicated solely to Jockey distribution operations.

Healthcare Logistics

Managed by dedicated divisions like Leo Healthcare and Leo Logistics, ensuring seamless supply chain management throughout Kerala.

Corporate medical equipment distribution for GE HealthCare in Kerala is primarily managed by regional medical equipment dealers like Wipro GE Healthcare (which has branch offices in Kochi and Thrissur).
