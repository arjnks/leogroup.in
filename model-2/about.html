Our Legacy.

Chapter 01

The Beginning

From 250 sq.ft to a market leader.

Our journey to becoming the market leader started in 1974 when (Late) Mr. T.S. Pattabhiraman (B.Tech IIT Madras, MBA IISc Bangalore), Mr. T.S. Anantharaman (B.Com, FCA), and Mr. T.S. Rajaram (B.Sc, FCA) commenced a partnership firm with just two employees and 250 sq.ft of rented space. Being highly ambitious and resourceful, the fundamentals of the business were established right at the beginning, helping the group grow enormously over the years.

Chapter 02

Scale & Scope

A conglomerate driving Kerala's supply chain.

With Mr. Sidharth Ram at the helm of affairs since 2005, Leo Group is now a conglomerate of multiple private limited companies and partnership firms providing direct employment to over 600 individuals. We have invested heavily in creating world-class infrastructure, currently owning 2 Lakh Sq.Ft of state-of-the-art warehousing. All these are an affirmation of our position as the premier market leader in warehousing and distribution in Kerala.
